General Maintenance

At Genkada Automobile, our General Maintenance service is the foundation of vehicle longevity. We ensure every car runs like new—reliable, efficient, and ready for the road.

Our Maintenance Expertise

Our General Maintenance services cover everything your car needs to perform at its best—oil changes, engine tune-ups, brake servicing, fluid replacements, suspension alignment, battery servicing, electrical diagnostics, tire balancing, AC servicing, and routine inspections.

We use only high-quality OEM parts, advanced tools, and a team of skilled technicians to diagnose and restore your vehicle with precision. Every vehicle receives a custom maintenance strategy depending on its model, age, and usage.

Real Impact. Real Results.

Clients report improved fuel economy, smoother acceleration, noise reduction, and better resale value after Genkada maintenance. We have transformed over 700+ vehicles from struggling to stunning condition through committed service delivery.

Why Choose Genkada?

We prioritize customer satisfaction, transparency, and a track record of reliable maintenance. Our mechanics are certified professionals with years of experience in handling cars from Toyota, Mercedes-Benz, Honda, Ford, and more.

Service Promise

We guarantee detailed vehicle inspection, honest diagnostics, cost-effective solutions, and timely delivery. We don’t just maintain cars—we upgrade their entire experience.
