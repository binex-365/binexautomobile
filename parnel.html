Panel Beating & Body Repairs

We turn damaged, dented, and rough vehicles into sleek, showroom-ready cars. Trust Genkada’s expert panel beaters to restore perfection.

What We Offer

Our panel beating service is comprehensive and precise—covering everything from minor dents to full-body accident repairs. We realign frames, fix creased panels, remove rust, and replace damaged parts using modern techniques like dent pulling, welding, filling, and surface treatment.

We use specialized tools and industry-grade machinery to ensure structural alignment and factory-level finish. Every vehicle leaves our facility looking brand new—safely and beautifully.

Why Choose Genkada?

With over 800+ successful panel jobs completed, we’ve helped clients restore cars after collisions, hail damage, theft recoveries, and more. Our results speak for themselves.

Our Process

From inspection to delivery, we follow a strict process: diagnosis, disassembly, frame straightening, body repair, priming, sanding, painting, and final inspection. Each car is handled with extreme care and documented for transparency.

We also handle insurance claims and provide free consultations for accident-damaged cars. Your safety and satisfaction is our goal. All repairs are logged and reviewed by senior technicians.

We work on sedans, SUVs, trucks, buses, and luxury cars. From minor touch-ups to major body reconstruction—Genkada is the trusted name in auto body perfection.
