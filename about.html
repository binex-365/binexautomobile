Welcome to Genkada Automobile

Your trusted partner for car sales, maintenance, and automotive solutions in Nigeria and Africa. We are dedicated to making car ownership joyful, affordable, and reliable for everyone.

Who We Are

Genkada Automobile is a forward-thinking automotive company based in Nigeria, committed to transforming the way people buy, sell, and maintain vehicles. Our platform connects buyers, sellers, and service providers, ensuring transparency, trust, and satisfaction at every step.

  • Registered and trusted in the Nigerian automotive industry.
  • Experienced team of professionals, mechanics, and customer care agents.
  • Wide network of partners and verified dealers across the country.
  • Modern digital platform for seamless transactions and support.

What We Offer

  • Car Sales: New and used cars, SUVs, and trucks from trusted sources.
  • Maintenance & Repairs: Certified workshops and mobile mechanics for all brands.
  • Car Inspection: Pre-purchase and routine inspection services for peace of mind.
  • Financing & Insurance: Flexible payment plans and insurance options.
  • Trade-In & Upgrade: Swap your old car for a better one easily.
  • Roadside Assistance: Emergency help when you need it most.

Why Choose Genkada Automobile?

  • Trust & Transparency: Every transaction is open, honest, and secure.
  • Customer-Centric: We put your needs first, always.
  • Innovation: We use technology to make your experience better and easier.
  • Community Impact: We support local jobs, skills, and social initiatives.
  • Affordable Solutions: Quality cars and services at prices you can trust.

Meet Our Team

Our passionate team includes automotive experts, engineers, digital innovators, and customer care professionals. We are united by a shared vision: to make car ownership and mobility better for everyone.

More About Us

  • We are always evolving—your feedback helps us grow!
