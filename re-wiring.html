Vehicle Re-Wiring Services

Precision, safety, and reliability—Genkada Automobile delivers expert automotive rewiring services for all vehicle models.

What We Do

Rewiring is more than replacing wires—it's restoring your vehicle’s heartbeat. At Genkada, we identify faulty circuits, worn-out connections, burnt relays, and outdated installations. Our re-wiring specialists use industry-standard tools and high-quality wire looms to deliver safe and neat wiring layouts.

Whether it's full rewiring, ECU troubleshooting, dashboard lighting, headlight harness replacement, or fixing shorts and blown fuses, our service is thorough, professional, and cleanly executed.

Why Genkada?

Our clients trust us because we blend deep electrical knowledge with care and precision. We ensure every repair passes our rigorous quality and insulation checks—ensuring durability and peak performance.

Our Guarantee

Every rewire job comes with a post-inspection report and service warranty. We also guide car owners on how to prevent future wiring issues. Our solutions are ideal for cars with electrical issues, upgraded sound systems, lighting mods, or engine swaps.

At Genkada, our wiring professionals provide detailed before-and-after diagnostics and label every connection point for long-term maintenance. We also assist with aftermarket installations, reverse camera wiring, advanced fuse board upgrades, and complex engine harness replacements.
