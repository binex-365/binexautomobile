Vehicle Painting Services

From complete color changes to precision touch-ups, Genkada Automobile delivers premium automotive painting with glowing finishes and perfect shine.

Our Expertise

Painting is not just about color—it's about restoration, protection, and transformation. Our painting specialists apply professional-grade primers, base coats, and clear coats to ensure your car looks as vibrant and flawless as the day it left the factory.

Whether it’s full-body resprays, color change projects, scratch repairs, oxidation removal, or custom designs—we combine art and engineering for a jaw-dropping finish every time.

Why Paint With Genkada?

We operate with a dust-free paint booth, computerized color matching, and eco-friendly coatings. Every paint job is backed by a finish warranty and post-spray inspection. Our experts handle classic restorations, metallic finishes, matte styles, and even exotic car repaints with care and precision.

Our Process

We begin with a detailed inspection and prep phase—stripping old paint, sanding the surface, repairing imperfections, and masking sensitive areas. Then comes base coating, curing, and polishing for a showroom-level result.

Genkada also offers pearl, matte, gloss, and candy finishes. We’re equipped for exotic car painting, brand color matches, and commercial fleet designs. We provide regular updates throughout the job so you always know what’s happening with your vehicle.

Clients also benefit from our optional rust prevention undercoating, ceramic topcoats for durability, and multi-tone layouts for sports cars and show vehicles.
